# S21 ROOFER - Audio Walkthrough Script
**Duration: ~2-3 minutes**

---

## OPENING (10 seconds)

Welcome to S21 ROOFER - your intelligent field assistant for roofing sales and insurance claims. Let me walk you through how to access and use each feature.

---

## LOGIN (15 seconds)

To get started, open the app and enter your email address on the login screen. Click "Continue with Email" and check your inbox. Click the magic link in the email, and you'll be logged in instantly - no password needed.

---

## HOME SCREEN OVERVIEW (20 seconds)

Once logged in, you'll see the home screen with quick action buttons at the top and a full feature list below. The four main quick actions are: Start Chat for instant AI help, Upload and Analyze for documents and photos, Generate Email for professional communication, and Insurance Directory to find insurance company contacts.

---

## CHAT ASSISTANT (20 seconds)

Tap "Start Chat" to talk with S21 - your AI strategic ally. Ask anything about roofing, insurance claims, GAF products, or building codes. S21 searches through 130 documents and gives you word-for-word scripts, complete battle plans, and proven strategies. It's like having an expert in your pocket.

---

## UPLOAD & ANALYZE (20 seconds)

Tap "Upload and Analyze" to scan insurance documents, approvals, denials, or roof photos. Upload the file, and S21 instantly analyzes it - pulling out key details, identifying issues, and giving you exactly what to say or do next. Perfect for quick decisions in the field.

---

## VOICE TRANSCRIPTION (15 seconds)

The Voice Transcription tool converts your voice notes into text. Record your thoughts after a meeting or inspection, and it automatically transcribes everything - saving you time on documentation.

---

## EMAIL GENERATOR (15 seconds)

Need to email a customer or insurance company? Tap "Generate Email." Tell S21 what you need, and it creates a professional, ready-to-send email with the right tone and all the details.

---

## INSURANCE DIRECTORY (15 seconds)

The Insurance Directory gives you instant access to over 50 insurance companies - complete with contact info and direct links to their claims portals. No more searching around.

---

## LIVE MODE (20 seconds)

Live Mode is your real-time voice assistant. Tap it before a customer meeting, and Susan - S21's voice - listens and gives you instant tactical guidance. Ask a question out loud, and she responds with exactly what to say. Double-tap the mic to stop her if needed. It's like having a coach in your ear.

---

## AGNES - OBJECTION HANDLER (25 seconds)

Agnes helps you crush objections. She has scripts for 19 common objections like "too expensive," "got a denial," or "need to think about it." Tap any objection and get word-for-word scripts, psychology breakdowns, and backup strategies.

Agnes also has Roleplay Mode - switch to it, and Agnes becomes the tough customer. You practice your pitch, she pushes back hard, and then coaches you on what worked. It's the best way to build confidence.

---

## DOCUMENT JOB (15 seconds)

Document Job lets you create professional job reports. Add photos, notes, and details about the project, and generate a clean report you can share with customers or your team.

---

## KNOWLEDGE BASE (15 seconds)

The Knowledge Base gives you direct access to all 130 documents in S21's library - GAF product specs, building codes, insurance templates, and sales scripts. Search for what you need and get instant answers.

---

## NAVIGATION (10 seconds)

Use the bottom navigation bar to switch between features anytime. Tap the home icon to return to the main screen. Your chat history and documents are always saved.

---

## CLOSING (10 seconds)

That's S21 ROOFER - your complete field assistant. Login with email, access any feature from the home screen, and let S21 help you close more deals. Get started today.

---

**Total Time: ~2 minutes 45 seconds**
**Word Count: ~575 words**
