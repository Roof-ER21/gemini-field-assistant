## Troubleshooting

### "NotebookLM won't accept my file"
- **Solution:** The file is a .md (markdown) file. If it won't upload, rename it to .txt
- Or copy/paste the content into NotebookLM's text input

### "Audio is too long"
- **This is normal!** Comprehensive training = longer audio
- **Solution:** Use chapter breakdown to create shorter segments
- Or speed up playback to 1.25x or 1.5x

### "I want different voices or style"
- **NotebookLM chooses voices automatically**
- **Can't change:** Voice selection is automatic
- **Alternative:** Use the audio as a script and record your own

### "Can I edit the script before generating?"
- **Yes!** Edit S21_ROOFER_VIDEO_SCRIPT.md before uploading
- Remove chapters you don't need
- Add company-specific information
- Adjust examples to your market
