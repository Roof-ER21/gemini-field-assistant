# Notifications User Guide

## How to Use Notifications

### Viewing Notifications

#### Desktop
1. Look for the **bell icon** in the top-right corner of the sidebar
2. A **red badge** will show the number of unread notifications
3. Click the bell to open the notifications panel
4. Scroll through your notifications

#### Mobile
1. Look for the **bell icon** next to the menu button in the header
2. A **red badge** will show the number of unread notifications
3. Tap the bell to open the notifications panel
4. Swipe to scroll through your notifications

### Managing Notifications

#### Mark as Read
- Click **"Mark all as read"** button at the top of the panel
- All notifications will be marked as read
- The badge will disappear

#### Refresh
- Click the **refresh icon** to manually update your notifications
- Notifications auto-refresh every 60 seconds
- Real-time updates via WebSocket (no refresh needed)

#### Close Panel
- Click the **X button** in the top-right of the panel
- Or click anywhere outside the panel

### Real-Time Updates

Notifications appear **instantly** when:
- Someone sends you a message
- Someone mentions you
- Someone shares content with you

**No page refresh needed!** The bell badge and notification list update automatically.

### Tips

1. **Keep the app open** for instant notifications via WebSocket
2. **Check regularly** - the badge shows your unread count
3. **Mark as read** to keep your notification list clean
4. **Click notifications** to see details (future feature)

### Troubleshooting

#### Not receiving notifications?
1. Check you're logged in
2. Refresh the page
3. Check your internet connection
4. Contact your admin if issues persist

#### Badge not updating?
1. Wait 60 seconds for auto-refresh
2. Click the refresh button in notifications panel
3. Refresh the page
