# S21 ROOFER - Complete User Guide & Feature Overview

## What is S21 ROOFER?

S21 ROOFER (S21 Field AI) is an intelligent field assistant designed specifically for roofing sales representatives. It combines multiple AI systems to help reps with customer interactions, documentation, objection handling, and administrative tasks - all from one unified platform.

### 8. Agnes - Objection Handler üí™

**What it does:**
Your specialized AI coach for handling customer objections. Get word-for-word scripts for common sales objections with proven psychology-based approaches.

**Key Features:**
- 6 quick objection buttons for instant help
- Custom objection input for any scenario
- Exact scripts to use (word-for-word)
- Explanation of why each approach works
- Backup strategies if customer still resists
- Based on proven sales psychology

**Common objections covered:**
1. **"Too expensive"** - Price objections
2. **"Need to think about it"** - Timing/hesitation
3. **"Got a denial from insurance"** - Insurance denials
4. **"Insurance only paid partial"** - Partial payment issues
5. **"Want to cancel early"** - Cancellation requests
6. **"Already got other quotes"** - Competition objections

**How to use:**
1. Click "Handle Objection" from home page Quick Reference
2. Click a quick objection button OR type your custom objection
3. Agnes provides:
   - üìù **What to Say:** Exact script to use
   - üí° **Why It Works:** Sales psychology explanation
   - üéØ **If They Still Resist:** Backup approach
4. Use the script in your conversation
5. Adjust to your personal style

**Example response format:**
```
Customer says: "It's too expensive"

üìù What to Say:
"I completely understand your concern, and many of our best customers felt the same way initially. But here's what they discovered - when you factor in our lifetime warranty, the premium materials that last 50% longer, and the fact that we handle all insurance paperwork saving you 20+ hours of hassle, the real question becomes: can you afford NOT to invest in quality? Let me show you exactly how this pays for itself..."

üí° Why It Works:
This validates their concern while reframing from cost to value. It uses social proof ("best customers felt the same"), introduces scarcity (warranty, materials), and shifts the conversation to long-term ROI rather than upfront price.

üéØ If They Still Resist:
Ask: "What's your biggest concern about the investment - the upfront cost, or the monthly payment structure? We have financing options that make this more affordable than you might think." Then address their specific concern directly with numbers.
```
