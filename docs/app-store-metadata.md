## 4. Description
**4000 characters max**

```
ROOFER S21 is the ultimate AI-powered field assistant built exclusively for roofing professionals. Powered by Google Gemini AI and backed by 120+ industry documents, S21 delivers instant answers, smart analysis, and professional tools right when you need them most - in the field.

INTELLIGENT AI CHAT
Ask S21 anything about roofing sales, products, installation, or insurance claims. Get accurate answers with citations from our extensive knowledge base including GAF product guides, sales scripts, and industry best practices. S21 speaks your language and understands the roofing business inside and out.

DOCUMENT & PHOTO ANALYSIS
Upload inspection photos, damage documentation, or insurance paperwork. S21's AI vision capabilities analyze images to identify damage types, assess conditions, and provide professional assessments. Turn field photos into actionable insights in seconds.

VOICE TRANSCRIPTION
Record customer conversations, inspection notes, or meeting highlights. S21 transcribes your voice recordings accurately, making documentation effortless. Review and share transcripts instantly without manual note-taking.

PROFESSIONAL EMAIL GENERATION
Draft polished emails to homeowners, insurance adjusters, and suppliers in seconds. Choose from templates or let S21 craft custom messages based on your situation. Sound professional every time without spending hours writing.

COMPREHENSIVE KNOWLEDGE BASE
Access your entire document library anywhere. Search through product specifications, installation manuals, warranty information, sales training materials, and company policies. Everything you need, instantly searchable.

JOB MANAGEMENT
Track and manage your roofing jobs from initial contact to completion. Keep all job details, photos, and notes organized in one place. Never lose track of a project again.

TEAM MESSAGING
Stay connected with your colleagues in real-time. Send messages, share photos, and coordinate on jobs without leaving the app. Built-in notification badges keep you updated.

INSURANCE COMPANY DIRECTORY
Quick access to insurance company contacts and claim procedures. Find the information you need to navigate the claims process efficiently.

LIVE MODE
Real-time AI interaction for when you need immediate answers. Perfect for customer meetings, inspections, and quick lookups.

BUILT FOR ROOFING PROFESSIONALS
- Roofing contractors and crews
- Sales representatives
- Estimators and project managers
- Insurance claim specialists
- Property inspectors

S21 is your knowledgeable colleague who never takes a day off. Whether you need product specs, sales advice, claim guidance, or professional communication, S21 has your back.

SECURITY & PRIVACY
Your data is protected with secure authentication. All communications are encrypted, and you maintain full control over your information.

REQUIREMENTS
- Active ROOFER S21 account required
- Contact your organization administrator for access credentials
- Internet connection required for AI features

Built by The Roof Docs for roofing professionals who demand excellence.
```
