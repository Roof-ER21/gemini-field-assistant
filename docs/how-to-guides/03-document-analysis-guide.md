## How to Analyze Documents

### Step 1: Upload Files
- **Drag and drop** files into the upload area
- Or **click to browse** and select files
- Upload multiple files at once
- Supported: JPG, PNG, PDF, DOCX, XLSX, CSV

### Step 2: Add Context (Optional)
```
"These are photos of hail damage from the May 15th storm.
Property at 123 Main St. Customer claims 20+ impact points.
Need damage assessment for State Farm claim #12345."
```

### Step 3: Click "Analyze Documents"
- AI processes all uploaded files
- Usually takes 10-30 seconds
- Shows progress indicator

### Step 4: Review Analysis
- Read AI-generated insights
- Check damage assessment
- Review key findings
- Note recommendations

### Step 5: Use Results
- Copy analysis to clipboard
- Include in insurance claims
- Share with customers
- Save for records

## Pro Tips

### Better Photos = Better Analysis

‚úÖ **Do:**
- Take multiple angles
- Get close-ups of damage
- Include overall roof shots
- Good lighting (avoid shadows)
- Include reference objects for scale
- Photograph in good weather

‚ùå **Don't:**
- Blur or poor quality
- Too far away
- Bad lighting
- Only one angle
- No context shots

### Add Helpful Context

Include:
- Date of damage/storm
- Property address
- Claim numbers
- Specific concerns
- What you're looking for
- Any customer notes

### Multiple Files

Upload:
- All damage photos at once
- Related documents together
- Before + after comparisons
- Multiple sides of roof
