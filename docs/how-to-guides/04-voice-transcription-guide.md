## Pro Tips

### For Better Transcriptions

‚úÖ **Do:**
- Speak clearly
- Minimize background noise
- Hold phone steady
- Test microphone first
- Record in quiet environment

‚ùå **Don't:**
- Mumble or speak too fast
- Record in windy conditions
- Cover microphone
- Record with loud machinery nearby

### What to Record

**Good recordings:**
- Customer consultations
- Inspection walkthroughs
- Phone conversations
- Adjuster meetings
- Team discussions

**Not ideal:**
- Very short notes (type instead)
- Extremely noisy environments
- Multiple overlapping conversations
