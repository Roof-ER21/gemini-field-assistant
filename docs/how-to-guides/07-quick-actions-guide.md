# How to Use Quick Actions - Fast Access to Common Tasks

## What is Quick Actions?

Quick Actions gives you instant access to the 3 most common tasks from anywhere in the app. Perfect for when you need something fast!

---

## How to Access

### Mobile (Easiest):
- Click **"+ Quick Actions"** button (bottom right of screen)
- Always visible, always accessible

### Desktop:
- Click Quick Actions from sidebar
- Or use the floating button
