Three Unfair Advantages

1. Multi-Source Cross-Verification
  • Every date stamps which sources confirm it (NOAA, NEXRAD, SPC, NWS, CoCoRaHS, IEM). Adjusters can independently verify each source.
  • IHM + HailTrace rebrand one or two sources; we show the corroboration trail.
2. Swath-First Direct Hit Labeling
  • If the property sits inside an MRMS hail-swath polygon on that date → DIRECT HIT. The radar-derived footprint, not a nearest-point approximation.
  • Matches the colored bands reps show the homeowner on the map.
3. Live Radar + Auto-Push
  • LIVE layer updates every 15 min. Reps see the storm while it's happening.
  • Sales Team auto-alert when ≥0.5" MESH detected in VA/MD/PA (test-group active; Sales Team on deck).
4. The "IHM has dates we don't" gap is closed
  • Every IHM-reported date for 145 DMV/PA cities materialized into our DB as source_ihm=true.
  • Rep facing IHM pushback? Every quoted date is already in our system — with every other source that confirms it.
