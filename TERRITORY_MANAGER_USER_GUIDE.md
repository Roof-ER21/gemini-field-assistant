# Territory Manager - User Guide

## Accessing Territory Manager

1. **From Sidebar**: Click "Territories" in the navigation menu
2. **From Mobile**: Tap the menu button, then select "Territories"

## Main Features

### 1. Territory Dashboard

When you open Territory Manager, you'll see:

- **Stats Overview** (top of page)
  - Total number of territories assigned to you
  - Total leads across all territories
  - Total appointments set

- **Your Territories** (main section)
  - List of all territories you own or are assigned to
  - Each territory shows:
    - Name and description
    - Assigned owner
    - Color-coded border
    - Coverage percentage
    - Leads, appointments, and lead conversion rate

- **Leaderboard** (right sidebar on desktop, below on mobile)
  - Top 10 performing territories
  - #1 territory highlighted in red
  - Shows territory name, owner, leads, and coverage

### 2. Check-In to a Territory

**Purpose**: Track when field reps are actively working in a territory

**How to Check In:**
1. Find the territory you want to work in
2. Click the "Check In" button
3. Allow location access if prompted (optional but recommended)
4. You'll see an "Active Check-In" banner appear at the top

**Active Check-In Banner Shows:**
- Duration of your current session (updates automatically)
- "Check Out" button to end your session

**Rules:**
- You can only be checked into ONE territory at a time
- Checking into a new territory automatically checks you out of the previous one
- Your location is captured for analytics (if you allow it)

### 3. Check-Out from a Territory

**When to Check Out:**
- When you're done working in the territory
- Before moving to a different territory
- End of your shift

**Check-Out Process:**
1. Click "Check Out" in the active check-in banner
2. You'll be prompted to enter your activity stats:
   - **Doors Knocked**: Total doors you knocked on
   - **Contacts Made**: People you actually spoke with
   - **Leads Generated**: Qualified leads you created
   - **Appointments Set**: Appointments scheduled
   - **Notes** (optional): Any additional notes about your session

3. Your stats are saved and the leaderboard updates

### 4. Territory Statistics

Each territory card shows real-time stats:

- **Coverage %**: Percentage of addresses canvassed in this territory
- **Leads**: Total leads generated in this territory
- **Appts**: Total appointments set
- **Lead Rate %**: Conversion rate (leads รท addresses canvassed)

### 5. Leaderboard

**What it shows:**
- Top 10 territories ranked by performance
- Territory name and assigned owner
- Total leads and coverage percentage

**Ranking factors:**
- Total leads generated
- Coverage percentage
- Overall activity

**#1 Position:**
- Highlighted with special red background
- Shows top performer across the organization

## Mobile App Notes

When using on iOS via Capacitor:

1. **Location Access**:
   - First time you check in, you'll be asked for location permission
   - This helps track which areas you're working in
   - You can still check in if you deny location access

2. **Background Activity**:
   - Keep the app open while checked in for accurate duration tracking
   - The timer continues even if you switch apps briefly

3. **Offline Mode**:
   - Check-ins require internet connection
   - If offline, wait until you have signal to check in/out

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Check In Immediately**: Check in as soon as you arrive at a territory
2. **Accurate Stats**: Enter accurate numbers when checking out for better analytics
3. **Regular Updates**: Check the leaderboard to see how your territory is performing
4. **Notes**: Use the notes field to record important observations or challenges

## Troubleshooting

**Can't Check In?**
- Make sure you're not already checked into another territory
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure you have territories assigned to you

**Leaderboard Not Showing Data?**
- Check-out at least once to populate leaderboard stats
- Refresh the page if data seems stale

**Location Errors?**
- Location is optional - you can still check in without it
- Check your device location settings if you want to enable it
- On iOS, go to Settings > Privacy > Location Services

**Stats Not Updating?**
- Make sure you completed the check-out process
- Refresh the Territory Manager page
- Check your internet connection

## Privacy & Data

- **Location Data**: Only captured if you grant permission, used for analytics only
- **Activity Stats**: Only you and admins can see your individual stats
- **Leaderboard**: Shows territory performance, not individual performance

## Need Help?

Contact your administrator if:
- You can't see any territories (you may not be assigned yet)
- Check-in/out features aren't working
- You need territories added or modified
