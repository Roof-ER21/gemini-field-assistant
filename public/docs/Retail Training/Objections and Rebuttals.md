# Objections & Rebuttals

## Framework: A.E.R.
- **A** - Agreeing + Right?
- **E** - Empathize (We know...)
- **R** - Redirect (My Job Is Simple)

After every rebuttal, go STR8 into the Re-Intro.

---

## "I have to talk to my spouse." (C.O.W.S)
"Makes sense.. The [Product], that's something you guys will want to talk about together, right? To be honest, that's actually what my job IS to do. My job is simple, I'll just get your name, a time that works for both you AND YOUR spouse, and I'll leave you with a flyer."

→ STR8 Into Re-Intro

---

## "I'm busy." (Not Now/Busy)
"Totally get it, busy schedule, right? Well, to be honest with you, that's exactly why I'm here… My job is simple, I'll just get your name, I'll help you find a time that ACTUALLY works around your busy schedule (Times: 10AM-7:30, ALSO Saturdays), and I'll leave you with a flyer."

→ STR8 Into Re-Intro

---

## "I don't have the money right now." (No $$$)
"Makes sense, the [Product] is going to have to wait for a little while, right? To be honest with you, that's actually why they send us out here. We're not looking to do the work today (lol) — just while my guys are in the area, we are going to leave everyone with that free information on like styles and prices, specific to your home, and we're gonna guarantee that quote in writing for the future; so that way when the time does come, that you want a real look at the [Product] you'll have a price on file with a professional company, that hasn't been touched by inflation, and you can use that to shop around and see who gives you the best deal, but our hope is you think of us."

…"FAIR ENOUGH?"

---

## "I'm not really looking into this any time soon, maybe come back next year." (Long-Term)
Use the "No Money" rebuttal — same principle applies. Lock in the price now, use it when ready.

---

## Key Principles
- Never argue — AGREE first ("Makes sense", "Totally get it")
- Always redirect to "My Job Is Simple" (3 steps)
- Frame the quote as a GIFT that protects against inflation
- Include spouse in scheduling, don't fight the objection
- Use humor to break tension ("We're not looking to do the work today lol")
