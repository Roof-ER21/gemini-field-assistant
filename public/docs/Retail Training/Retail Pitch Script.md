# Retail Pitch Script

## The Opening
Hello, how are you?
"Eh, you know, working lol" (Break Ice) {Chuckle}
"You look ________, I'll be quick"

My name is ___________.

I'm just giving the neighbors a heads up.

Our company is about to do the (product) for one of your neighbors. (POINT)

So, before we get going, we are going to come by, and we are going to do free quotes.

So far, we're coming by for everybody in the afternoons, or are the evenings better for you?

Great! My job is simple.
1. I get your name
2. I'll find a time that works best for you
3. Leave you with a flyer

## Re-Introduction (After Objection Handled)
"So like I said, my name is __________. I'm just giving the neighbors a heads up. Our company is about to do the (product) for one of your neighbors, and before we get going we're gonna come by and do free quotes. My job is simple: I get your name, find a time that works, and leave you with a flyer."

## Key Principles
- Break ice immediately with humor
- Keep it under 30 seconds
- Point to the neighbor's house for social proof
- Use assumptive close: "afternoons or evenings?"
- Frame as information delivery, not a sales call
- Always end with "My job is simple" (3 steps)
