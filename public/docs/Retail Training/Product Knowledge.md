# Product Knowledge — Full Roof-ER Product Line

## 1. Roof Replacement
**Why Replace?** Solves missing shingles, curling, and water intrusions. Prevents costly interior damage.
- **Comfort**: Proper ventilation eliminates excess heat and humidity. Without it, your warranty may be voided.
- **Curb Appeal**: Black streaks (algae), missing shingles, warped plywood destroy appearance. New roof increases resale value 2-4%.
- **Warranty**: 50 Year Non Pro-rated, 30 Years Workmanship, Algae/Stain Protection, Unlimited Wind Speed, Class 4 Hail. Master Elite and President Club status with GAF (top 0.01% nationwide).
- **Tagline**: ROOF REPLACEMENT MADE SIMPLE.

## 2. Solar
**Why Solar?** Lowers electricity bills and reduces reliance on the power company.
- **Tax Incentives**: Access to Solar Tax Professionals who maximize Federal and local incentives.
- **Net Metering**: Earn credits for excess energy sent back to the grid.
- **Home Value**: Increases home value 4-6%. Homes with solar sell faster.
- **Tagline**: CLEAN ENERGY SAVINGS MADE SIMPLE.

## 3. Solar PPA (Power Purchase Agreement)
**Why PPA?** Makes solar accessible with ZERO UPFRONT INVESTMENT.
- **Clean Energy Access**: Reduce carbon footprint without major investment.
- **Net Metering Credits**: Earn credits for excess energy.
- **Flexible Options**: At contract end: extend, purchase the system, or have it removed.
- **Tagline**: CLEAN ENERGY MADE SIMPLE.

## 4. Window Replacement
**Why Replace Windows?** Energy efficient windows lower bills by 10-30%.
- **Comfort**: Eliminates drafts, cold spots, humidity issues, allergens. Prevents mold growth.
- **Curb Appeal**: Enhance appearance with new exterior trim. Raises resale value 2-4%.
- **Warranty**: Lifetime warranty for the life of the PERSON, not the product. Last windows you'll ever buy.
- **Tagline**: WINDOW REPLACEMENT MADE SIMPLE.

## 5. Vinyl Siding
**Why Replace?** Failing sheeting, insulation, and vapor barriers lead to leaks, drafts, mold, uneven settling.
- **Comfort**: Proper insulation saves money, increases comfort, prevents HVAC strain. Proper house wrap prevents leaks and mold.
- **Curb Appeal**: Wood needs repainting, aluminum dents, vinyl is typically low-grade. New siding upgrades everything.
- **Warranty**: TRUE Lifetime warranty — covers the lifetime of the OWNER, not a set number of years.
- **Tagline**: SIDING REPLACEMENT MADE SIMPLE.
