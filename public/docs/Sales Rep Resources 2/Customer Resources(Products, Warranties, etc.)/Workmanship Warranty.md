# Workmanship Warranty

## Roof-ER 10-Year Workmanship Warranty

Issued by: Roof-ER
Address: 2106 Gallows Rd Ste D, Vienna, VA 22182
Licensed in Virginia, Maryland, and Pennsylvania

---

## What This Warranty Covers

Roof-ER warrants that all roofing and related exterior work performed by our installation crews will be free from defects in workmanship for a period of ten (10) years from the date of substantial completion.

Covered workmanship defects include:

- Improper flashing installation that results in water intrusion
- Nail patterns or fastener placement that does not meet manufacturer specifications
- Improper underlayment installation or sealing
- Ridge cap installation defects
- Valley installation defects (open or closed valleys)
- Starter strip installation errors
- Improper sealing around penetrations (vents, pipes, skylights, chimneys) performed by Roof-ER crews
- Gutter and downspout installation defects (where installed by Roof-ER)
- Siding installation defects resulting in water infiltration (where Roof-ER performed the siding work)

If a covered defect causes water intrusion or structural damage, Roof-ER will repair the defect and any directly resulting interior damage at no charge to the homeowner.

---

## What This Warranty Does NOT Cover

This workmanship warranty does not cover:

- Defects in materials (these are covered separately by manufacturer warranties — GAF or CertainTeed, depending on the products installed)
- Damage caused by weather events, hail, wind, ice, or acts of nature occurring after installation
- Damage caused by homeowner or third-party modifications to the roof system
- Normal wear and aging of materials
- Damage caused by lack of maintenance (clogged gutters causing backup, failure to clear debris)
- Pre-existing structural defects not identified or disclosed at time of installation
- Damage caused by any contractor other than Roof-ER performing work on the same roof assembly after our installation
- Consequential or incidental damages beyond direct repair costs (lost income, hotel stays, contents damage)

---

## Manufacturer Warranties

Our workmanship warranty supplements, not replaces, manufacturer warranties. Depending on the product system installed:

- GAF systems: Standard limited warranty, plus enhanced warranties (Silver Pledge, Golden Pledge) available for qualifying installations
- CertainTeed systems: SureStart Plus warranty available through Roof-ER's ShingleMaster certification

Manufacturer warranties cover material defects. Roof-ER's workmanship warranty covers labor and installation. Both apply simultaneously.

## How to File a Workmanship Warranty Claim

1. Contact Roof-ER in writing (email preferred) at the address or contact information on your installation contract.
2. Describe the issue, the location on the roof, and when it was first noticed.
3. Attach photos if available.
4. Roof-ER will schedule a site inspection within 10 business days of receiving the written claim.
5. If the inspection confirms a covered workmanship defect, Roof-ER will schedule and complete the repair at no charge.
6. If the inspection determines the issue is not covered under workmanship warranty (e.g., storm damage, material defect), Roof-ER will advise you on the appropriate next step — manufacturer claim, insurance claim, or paid repair.

Emergency situations (active leak causing interior damage): Call our office directly. We will respond for emergency service within 24 hours and determine warranty coverage after the immediate issue is stabilised.

## Warranty Transferability

This workmanship warranty is transferable to a subsequent owner of the property under the following conditions:

- The transfer must be registered with Roof-ER in writing within 30 days of the property sale closing.
- The warranty transfers one time only. It does not transfer again to a third owner.
- The transferee assumes all remaining warranty terms and conditions.
- Roof-ER must receive proof of the property transfer (copy of closing settlement statement or deed).

Transferability is an active selling point for homeowners listing their property. A transferable 10-year workmanship warranty, combined with the manufacturer warranty, adds documented value to a real estate transaction.

---

## Conditions That Void This Warranty

This warranty is immediately voided if:

- A third-party contractor performs any roofing work on the same assembly without prior written consent from Roof-ER
- The homeowner makes modifications to the roof system (satellite dishes, solar panels, HVAC equipment) that penetrate the assembly without Roof-ER-approved sealing
- The property is used for commercial or non-residential purposes during the warranty period
- The homeowner fails to maintain adequate drainage (gutters, downspouts) causing preventable water backup under the roofing assembly

---

## Warranty Period

Ten (10) years from the date of substantial completion, as documented on the installation completion certificate provided at job close-out.

The warranty commencement date is the date Roof-ER certifies the installation complete — not the date materials were ordered or the project was contracted.

---

## Important Notes for Sales Reps

When presenting the warranty to homeowners:

- Always distinguish between workmanship warranty (Roof-ER) and material warranty (manufacturer). Homeowners often confuse the two.
- The 10-year workmanship warranty combined with a GAF or CertainTeed manufacturer warranty of 30 — lifetime years is a strong competitive differentiator.
- If the homeowner is close to selling their property, emphasise transferability.
- Never promise warranty coverage for damage categories that are excluded. Accuracy protects both the homeowner and Roof-ER.
