# Roof-ER CertainTeed ShingleMaster

## What Is ShingleMaster?

CertainTeed ShingleMaster is CertainTeed's highest-tier contractor certification. It is not automatic — it requires meeting and maintaining specific standards covering installation training, business practices, and volume. Only a fraction of roofing contractors in any market carry this designation. Roof-ER is a certified ShingleMaster contractor operating in VA/MD/PA.

ShingleMaster is the credential that unlocks CertainTeed's premium warranty tiers. Without it, those warranties are not available to the homeowner regardless of what products are installed.

---

## Requirements to Earn and Maintain ShingleMaster

To hold ShingleMaster status, a contractor must:

- Have at least two (2) crew members certified through CertainTeed's 4STAR Installer training program
- Maintain a minimum volume of CertainTeed product installations
- Hold current general liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage
- Carry a valid contractor's license in all states of operation
- Maintain a satisfactory reputation record (no unresolved complaints with CertainTeed)
- Renew certification annually — ShingleMaster status is not permanent and must be maintained

Roof-ER meets all of these requirements and holds active ShingleMaster status.

## Warranty Tiers Unlocked by ShingleMaster

CertainTeed's warranty program is tiered. ShingleMaster certification is required to offer the two premium tiers to customers.

### SureStart Plus Warranty (Standard — Available to All CertainTeed Contractors)
- Duration: Varies by product (typically 30-year to lifetime on premium shingles)
- Coverage: Material defects
- Labor: Not included after the first two years
- Transferable: Yes, to subsequent owner
- This is the baseline — ShingleMaster unlocks the enhanced tiers below

### 4-Star Warranty (Requires ShingleMaster)
- Duration: Lifetime on qualifying CertainTeed shingle products
- Coverage: Materials + 10 years labor
- Wind coverage: Up to 110 mph (Class D, ASTM D3161)
- Algae resistance: 10-year warranty if CertainTeed algae-resistant products used
- Transferable: Yes, one time, within 60 days of property sale
- Requirements: Installation must use CertainTeed's starter, underlayment, and ridge cap products alongside shingles

### 5-Star Warranty (Requires ShingleMaster + Full CertainTeed System)
- Duration: Lifetime on qualifying products
- Coverage: Materials + 25 years labor
- Wind coverage: Up to 130 mph (Class F, ASTM D3161)
- Algae resistance: 15-year warranty
- Transferable: Yes, one time, within 60 days of property sale
- Requirements: Full CertainTeed roofing system — shingles, CertaFlex underlayment, WinterGuard ice and water barrier, Ridge Cap, Starter Strip Plus. ALL components must be CertainTeed.
- This is the premium offering and the strongest competitive warranty in the CertainTeed program

## CertainTeed Product Standards Relevant to Claims

When using CertainTeed products for insurance claims, reference these standards in supplement letters and adjuster communications:

- ASTM D3161: Wind resistance testing standard. CertainTeed premium shingles meet Class F (130 mph) under this standard when installed per manufacturer spec.
- ASTM D7158: Additional wind uplift standard. Relevant for high-wind area code compliance claims.
- UL 2218 Class 4: Impact resistance. CertainTeed's Landmark IR and NorthGate products carry Class 4 rating — relevant when arguing for impact-resistant shingle upgrades in hail claims.
- UL Class A Fire Rating: All CertainTeed asphalt shingle products carry UL Class A fire rating.

## How to Use ShingleMaster in Sales

The ShingleMaster certification closes three common objections:

Objection: "I'll just use whoever the insurance company sends."
Response: Insurance companies don't send contractors. The homeowner chooses. And the contractor the homeowner chooses determines which warranty tier is available. A non-ShingleMaster contractor means no 5-Star warranty regardless of what products they install.

Objection: "Can't I just buy the warranty separately?"
Response: The 4-Star and 5-Star warranties are not purchasable — they require a ShingleMaster contractor. It is built into the installation, not added on afterward.

Objection: "What's the difference between your company and the other quote I have?"
Response: Ask if the other contractor is a CertainTeed ShingleMaster. If not, the homeowner is comparing a 10 or 25-year labor warranty against a 2-year labor warranty. That is not an apples-to-apples comparison.
