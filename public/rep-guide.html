PDF Reports

Storm Reports — Your Secret Weapon

One tap generates a professional, leave-behind PDF with federal storm data. Hand it to a homeowner. Email it to an adjuster. Use it on the door.

1
Search the Address
Type any address into Storm Maps. Susan pulls hail history, NWS warnings, and SPC storm reports instantly.
2
Review Storm Events
See dates, hail sizes, and damage radius. Tap an event to select it for the report.
3
Generate the PDF
Hit "Generate Report" — Susan creates a 4-page branded PDF in seconds using your rep profile information.
4
Share or Save
Download it, AirDrop it, email it directly to the homeowner or adjuster from within the app.
Pro Tip
Go to Settings > Profile and set your phone number before generating your first report — it gets embedded on the cover page.

Get Started

Quick Start Checklist

Seven steps to go from zero to closing. Tap each step as you complete it.

0 / 7 done
Step 01
Log in with your @theroofdocs.com email
Use your official company email — personal emails won't grant full access.
Step 02
Go to Settings > Profile and set your phone number
Your number is printed on every report cover page. Do this before your first PDF.
Step 03
Search your first address in Storm Maps
Try a current lead or your own home. See what the data looks like before you're in the field.
Step 04
Generate your first storm report PDF
Hit "Generate Report" from any storm event. Download it to your phone and open it — that's what the homeowner sees.
Step 05
Ask Susan a question in the chat
Try: "What does ACV mean?" or "How do I handle a denied claim?" Susan is trained on the full roofing process.
Step 06
Send a storm report to an adjuster
Use the Email tool to draft and send. Connect Gmail for one-tap sending with open tracking.
Step 07
Check Team Knowledge for field intel
Your team posts scripts, tips, and updates here. Read it before your first day in the field.
You're ready to work.
All 7 steps complete. Open the app and start finding storms.
